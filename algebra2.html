Algebra II

Algebra II is offered to students who have completed Algebra I. You will either take it in 9th grade or 10th grade.

Algebra II is an extension of the material covered in Algebra I, along with new topics covered in the area of Algebra. Students will take a final examination, as well as the Algebra I Regents in June.

Algebra II Curriculum

  1. Polynomials and rational expressions
  2. Functions
  3. Quadratic functions and equations
  4. Imaginary numbers and complex roots
  5. Polynomial functions
  6. Exponential functions
  7. Logarithmic functions
  8. Trigonometry
  9. Statistics and probability

Honors Criteria

Algebra II Honors covers everything from Algebra II, but in greater depth. It is offered to students who have completed Algebra I with an A or A+, or Algebra I Honors with a B- average minimum.
