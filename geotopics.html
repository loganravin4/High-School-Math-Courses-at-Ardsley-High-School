Geometry Topics

Geometry Topics is offered to students who have completed Algebra 1B or Algebra II, but may have had trouble doing so.

Geometry Topics is designed to do exactly what Geometry and Trigonometry does, except there are not as many topics to cover. You will explore more complex geometric situations, along with learning how to mathematically prove something. Select Algebra II & Trigonometry topics will be included in the course. There is a school final examination in June at the end of this course.
