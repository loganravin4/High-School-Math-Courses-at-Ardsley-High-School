High School Math Courses at Ardsley High School

What is Mathematics?

Mathematics is a subject that you are required to take in order to graduate high school. You need 3 credits total from math classes to graduate Ardsley High School.

This is good for you, because it can benefit you in the future. For example, statistics and probability can help you understand the main events of the world because sometimes, through statistics is the only way that an event can be described.

This website is designed to give students an overview of all of the mathematics courses offered at Ardsley High School to hopefully make their decision of what courses to take a bit easier.
