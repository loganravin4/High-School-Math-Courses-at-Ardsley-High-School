Algebra 1A & 1B

Algebra 1A & 1B are offered to students who may have struggled with Math 8.

Algebra 1A & 1B are the split of Algebra I into two years. It covers common core Algebra Topics in an extended format. After this, there is no need to take Algebra II. During Algebra 1B, students will take the common core regents examination in January.

Algebra 1A & 1B Modules

  1. Relationships Between Quantities and Reasoning with Equations and Their Graphs
  2. Descriptive Statistics
  3. Linear & Exponential Functions
  4. Polynomial and Quadratic Expressions, Equations, and Functions
  5. A Synthesis of Modeling with Equations and Functions
