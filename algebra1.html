Algebra I

Algebra I is offered to students who have either completed Single Accelerated Math in 7th Grade or regular Math 8. You either take it in 8th grade or 9th grade.

Algebra I formalizes and extends what students have learned throughout middle school. The critical areas deepen and extend understanding of mathematical relationships. Students will take a local final examination in June.

Algebra I Curriculum

  1. Foundations of Algebra
  2. Exponents
  3. Operations with Polynomials & Factoring
  4. Linear Equations
  5. Relations & Functions
  6. Linear Functions
  7. The Equation of Linear Functions
  8. Inequalities
  9. Systems of Equations & Inequalities
  10. Radicals
  11. Right Triangle Trigonometry
  12. Sets & Probability

Algebra I Honors is only offered to 8th graders who have maintained a B- average minimum in Single Accelerated Honors Math in the 7th grade. It is not offered to incoming 9th graders.
