Pre-Calculus

Pre-Calculus is offered to students who have completed either Geometry and Trigonometry or Geometry Topics. You will either take it in 11th grade or 12th grade.

Pre-Calculus is intended to provide the mathematical background needed for calculus. Topics include functions and their graphs, polynomial and rational functions, exponential and logarithmic functions, trigonometry, sequence and series, limits, and an introduction to differential calculus.

Pre-Calculus Curriculum

  1. Functions
  2. Polynomials
  3. Exponential and Logarithmic Functions
  4. Trigonometric Functions
  5. Vectors
  6. Systems of Equations
  7. Sequences and Series
  8. Parametric and Polar Equations

Honors Criteria

Pre-Calculus Honors covers everything in Pre-Calculus, along with continuity and derivatives. It is offered to students who have completed their Geometry Course with an A or A+, or Geometry and Trigonometry Honors with a B- average minimum.
