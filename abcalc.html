Advanced Placement Calculus AB

AP Calculus AB is offered to 12th graders who either want a challenge or found Pre-Calculus too easy.

Like Calculus, this is a traditional course in differential and integral calculus. The course is designed to prepare students for further studies of calculus at the college level, as well as prepares students for the Advanced Placement Calculus AB Exam.

AP Calculus AB Curriculum

  1. Limits and Continuity
  2. Differentiation: Definition and Fundamental Properties
  3. Differentiation: Composite, Implicit, and Inverse Functions
  4. Contextual Applications of Differentiation
  5. Analytical Applications of Differentiation
  6. Integration and Accumulation of Change
  7. Differential Equations
  8. Applications of Integration

How do I get offered this course?

To get offered a placement in AP Calculus AB, you must have completed Pre-Calculus with an A average, or Pre-Calculus Honors with a C average minimum.
